Interests

I am a Ph.D. student in Oceanography conentrating on coastal systems. My interests and research are focused on:

  • Coastal morphodynamics: Why are the coasts the shapes they are and how are they changing over time?

  • Nearshore Processes: How do waves, tides, currents, sea level variations (etc.) interact to drive coastal evolution?

  • Numerical Modeling: We cannot (yet) accurately predict how every grain of sand will move. Instead, can we find a useful balance of simple equations and parameterizations to effectively characterize more complex coastal realities?

  • Remote Sensing: If we cannot model perfectly, we still also need to make observations. How can we use remote sensing to better measure the many complex processes occurring simultaneously?

  • Research Application: There are simply too many unanswered questions to address at random. How can I and other scientists steer our scientific inquiries to address pertinent social and political needs while remaining objective?
