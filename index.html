Interests

I am a Ph.D. student in Oceanography conentrating on coastal systems. My interests and research are focused on:

  • Coastal morphodynamics: Why the the coasts the shapes they are? Why and how do they change over time?
  • Nearshore Processes: How do waves, tides, currents, sea level variations (etc.) interact to drive coastal evolution?
  • Numerical Modeling: Since we cannot predict how every grain of sand will move, can we find a useful balance of simple equations and parameterizations to effectively characterize real coastal processes?
  • Remote Sensing: There is A LOT going on in coastal environments. How can we use remote sensing to better measure the many complex processes occurring simultaneously?
  • Research Application: There are simply too many unanswered questions to address at random. How can I steer my scientific inquiries to address pertinent social and political needs?
